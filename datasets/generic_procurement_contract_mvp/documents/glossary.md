# Glossary

| Preferred Term | Other Terms in Dataset | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Purchase Request | PR, purchase requisition | Request before sourcing or contract |
| Supplier | Vendor | External provider of goods or services |
| Purchase Order | PO | Operational order released after contract activation |
| Goods Receipt | GRN, receipt record | Warehouse receiving evidence |
| Payment Request | Payment Application | Finance request to pay matched invoice |
| Department Manager | Dept Manager | Department-level approver |
| Finance Manager | Finance Approver | High-value PR and payment approver |
| Legal Reviewer | Legal Approver | Contract legal terms reviewer |
| Approval Record | Approval Step, decision record | Workflow decision evidence |
| Contract | Agreement | Legal agreement with supplier |

## Abbreviation Notes

- `PR` means Purchase Request in code and some sample data.
- `PO` means Purchase Order.
- `GRN` means Goods Receipt Note.
- `AP` means Accounts Payable.
